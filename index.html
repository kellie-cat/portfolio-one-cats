A compilation of photos, meowmories and stories about cats

Welcome to the Pet Pawtfolio, Thank you for taking an interest in my furbabies. Before we learn about their personal stories, I would like to share my reasons for loving black cats. They really have gotten me through some tough times and I would like to encourage anyone who has ever thought about adopting a black cat to feel confident that their lives will improve (most likely!)

  • Statistically, black cats are the healthiest and friendliest of all cat colours
  • They’re easy to match to any decor or outfit and tend to look sleek
  • These cats are intelligent and will spend hours unwittingly entertaining
  • Cats cheer you up when you are down - who doesn’t love a hot water bottle? No need for refills
  • Black kitties are basically mini house panthers
  • They have great contrast and are easy to find in the snow
  • Most importantly, they are less likely to be adopted and they need you!

Now that you have an idea of why I love cats, let’s meet mine!
